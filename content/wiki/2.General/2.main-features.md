# What are Secondary Features?

These are features not implemented with a major feature
Although they exist idependently, they still play a major role
A list of the main plugins will be available under the plugins page

# All Main Features

- **You can't craft Netherite armour and tools** <br>
You have to go towards the warzone, then to the right to a hut and trade for netherite gear
- **You can't craft end crystals** <br>
The only way of getting them is through the crates
- **NDI system** <br>
This system decides the power of a nations which allows for more fighters in a war 
- **Cannot Breed Villagers** <br>
You can only get villagers by trading for them with a trader. This is to lengthen the progression in the Server
- **AFK Area** <br>
You get a daily key every 15 minutes for standing still :D
