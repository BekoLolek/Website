# Vehicles

- Vehicles dont work for bedrock players
- The only way to get vechicles is through the level system and buying them directly by clicking the crate at spawn
- The Vehicles contribute towards the NDI system
- When placed, only the owner can pick them up
- The owner can be changed when the vehicle gets picked up with crouch + right click, then dropped to the other person
