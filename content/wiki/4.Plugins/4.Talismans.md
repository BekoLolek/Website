# Talismans

**What are they?** <br>
- Talismans are special power stones which give passive abilities
- Anyone can craft them, but you will need to reach the collections level for the effect to work
- You will not lose them when you die

**How to Use** <br>
- You have to do /Talismans and put them inside the inventory

**How to gain more slots** <br>
To begin, everyone has 3 slots <br>
- 5 = Level 10
- 7 = Level 25
- 10 = Level 50 / Knight Rank
- 12 = Level 70
- 14 = Level 80 / Baron Rank
- 16 = Level 100 / King Rank
- 18 = Level 125
- 20 = Level 150 / Emperor Rank

# How to Craft

**Levels**
- Each talisman has up to 3 levels
- For each level, there is a set template with only the N and P items changing depending on the talisman
- To craft greater levels, you'll need the previous talismans
- Each Talisman level is shown by 1, 2, 3 in the crafting recipies
