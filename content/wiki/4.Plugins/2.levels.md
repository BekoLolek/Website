# Levels

- Levels are the main priority of the server
- Currently, ths is the only way to get keys and other rewards
- You can see your level on the right side of the screen or when you do /levels
- To gain levels you need GlobalXP (GXP)

## How to get GXP

- Quests /Quests
- Collections

- As of now, there arn't many ways of getting GXP, but later this will expand

## Rewards

### Talismans
With the levels, you can upgrade your talisman capacity <br>
To begin, everyone has 3 slots <br>
- 5 = Level 10
- 7 = Level 25
- 10 = Level 50 / Knight Rank
- 12 = Level 70
- 14 = Level 80 / Baron Rank
- 16 = Level 100 / King Rank
- 18 = Level 125
- 20 = Level 150 / Emperor Rank
