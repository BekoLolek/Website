# Levels

- Levels are the main priority of the server
- Currently, ths is the only way to get keys and other rewards
- You can see your level on the right side of the screen or when you do /levels
- To gain levels you need GlobalXP (GXP)

## How to get GXP

- Quests /Quests
- Collections

- As of now, there arn't many ways of getting GXP, but later this will expand

## Rewards

### Talismans
With the levels, you can upgrade your talisman capacity <br>
To begin, everyone has 3 slots <br>
- 5 = Level 10
- 7 = Level 25
- 10 = Level 50 / Knight Rank
- 12 = Level 70
- 14 = Level 80 / Baron Rank
- 16 = Level 100 / King Rank
- 18 = Level 125
- 20 = Level 150 / Emperor Rank

### Keys
| Interval | Applies to levels | Rewards |
|---|---|---|
| Every 1 | Any level not caught by a rule below | Town Key |
| Every 3 | Multiples of 3 (not also 5/10/15/25/30/50/75/150) | Town Key, Country Key |
| Every 5 | Multiples of 5 (not also 10/15/25/30/50/75/150) | Town Key, Fun Vehicle Key |
| Every 10 | Multiples of 10 (not also 15/25/30/50/75/150) | Town Key, Fun Vehicle Key, World Key, Military Vehicle Key |
| Every 15 | Multiples of 15 (not also 25/30/50/75/150) | Town Key, Country Key, Fun Vehicle Key, QoL Vehicle Key |
| Every 25 | Multiples of 25 (not also 30/50/75/150) | Town Key, Fun Vehicle Key, Unreal Key |
| Every 30 | Multiples of 30 (not also 50/75/150) | Town Key, Country Key, Fun Vehicle Key, World Key, Military Vehicle Key, QoL Vehicle Key |
| Every 50 | Multiples of 50 (not also 75/150) | Town Key, Fun Vehicle Key, World Key, Military Vehicle Key, Unreal Key |
| Every 75 | Multiples of 75 (not also 150) | Town Key, Country Key, Fun Vehicle Key, QoL Vehicle Key, Unreal Key |
| Every 150 | Level 150 only | Town Key, Country Key, Fun Vehicle Key, World Key, Military Vehicle Key, QoL Vehicle Key, Unreal Key |
